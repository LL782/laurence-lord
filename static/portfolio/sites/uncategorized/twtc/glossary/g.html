Globalisation

Marx and Engels in "The Communist Manifesto" describes this process: "The need of a constantly expanding market for its products chases the bourgeoisie over the whole surface of the globe. |It must nestle everywhere, settle everywhere, establish connections everywhere." So Coca-Cola, MacDonalds, and, of course, C20th Fox aggressively seek their profits in every land and among every people. Note, despite the global capitalist system, the bourgeoisie still have a national base. So the most aggressive pursuit of global markets is that carried out by the USA. For Third Cinema the global Hollywood system is antagonistic both because they seek the same audience as well as because they advocate such different values.



Griot

A traditional African oral performer and/or storyteller. Also, a source of traditional wisdom and sometimes magic. This traditional performer appears in Sembène's "Xala" and "Ceddo". And Pays, the one tirailleur [Africans recruited as French colonial troops] who senses the coming massacre in "Camp de Thiaroye", is also a griot-type figure.
