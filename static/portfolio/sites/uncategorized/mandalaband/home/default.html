MANDALABAND III is the third reincarnation of an extraordinary progressive rock band with a long history. In fact, it has been thirty years since their last album (this must be a world record!)

The new album 'BC – Ancestors' is a masterpiece of symphonic rock, with majestic themes and mysterious soundscapes in the old Mandalaband style, but now enhanced by 21st-century sampling technology to create huge, complex, multi-layered sounds to envelop the senses. Twelve of Europe's finest musicians have come together to produce the most adventurous Prog Rock concept album of 2009. Two years in the making, BC – Ancestors is a must-have for all rock music aficionados.
